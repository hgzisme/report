*   Module 03-01-07: EC2 Auto Scaling
    *   Understood the EC2 Auto Scaling feature.
    *   It is used with specific conditions (scaling policies).
    *   Key points of EC2 Auto Scaling:
        *   Supports scaling the number of EC2 Instances up or down.
        *   Automatically registers EC2 Instances with an Elastic Load Balancer (ELB).
        *   Can operate across multiple AWS Availability Zones (AZs).
        *   Offers various Pricing Options.
