CloudFront is a CDN (Content Delivery Network) service from AWS. It helps reduce load for websites and applications by distributing content to locations closest to users.

CloudFront has recently updated its new interface, as well as new features and services.

In this workshop, we will learn how to configure CloudFront and use it to distribute content to locations closest to users.
